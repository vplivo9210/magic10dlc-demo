Verbal SMS Consent

Plivo's 10DLC campaign facilitates secure two-factor authentication (2FA) and targeted marketing communications via SMS. Messages are sent from Plivo-branded short codes or long codes, clearly identifying the sender as Plivo. The content includes verification codes for account security and promotional offers or updates relevant to Plivo services. All messages align with these purposes to ensure recipients receive timely and relevant information from Plivo.

Consent Process

By providing your phone number to plivo, you consent to receive text messages from us. This consent is obtained verbally during our interaction with you.

When you provide your phone number, you will be informed that: "By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from plivo. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time."
